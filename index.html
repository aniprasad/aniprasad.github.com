About Me

Hey there! I'm Anirudh and I live in Toronto. I graduated from UWaterloo with a degree in Computer Engineering in 2019. During my time there, I had the opportunity to intern at 6 different companies and work on a whole bunch of different things. More importantly, I met a ton of great folks along the way.


Currently, I work as a software developer at IBM Canada focusing on LLVM Compiler Development (along with a bit of infrastructure and automation work). I also co-lead the NextWave community at IBM which is focused on organizing networking and social events for early professional hires.


When I'm not working, I love playing the piano, ping pong and reading. Before the pandemic set in, I spent my free time hiking and exploring Toronto. I also dabble in simple automation scripts to make day to day tasks at home easier.

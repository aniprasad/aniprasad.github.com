About Me

Hey there! I'm Anirudh and I live in Toronto. I graduated from UWaterloo with a degree in Computer Engineering in 2019. I had the opportunity to intern at 6 different companies doing a whole bunch of different things, throughout my time there. Currently, I am working as a software developer at IBM Canada mostly focusing on LLVM Compiler Development (along with a bit of infrastructure work)

Growing up in quite a few countries has taught me a lot of lessons in diversity and the ability to adapt to various situations.
